Kejriwal Moseley Style

What you'll need...

...on hand

  • Two fat slices of thick sourdough
  • 80g hand gratedcheddar
  • 2 spring onions, chopped as you like
  • 1 green chilli (or 2, if you like it spicy), finely chopped
  • A glob of olive oil
  • 2 large, free range eggs
  • The black pepper they do in the coop with the grey lid
  • As much polish ketchup as it is physically possible to muster

...to do

  1. Don't bother pre-heating the oven, itĺl be fine
  2. Don't toast the bread
  3. Squash the cheese and spring onions together in a bowl, making sure to really smush it up. Coat the bastards in pepper too, the more the merrier.
  4. Put aside some of the beautiful cheese mix; you're going to cook eggs in it
  5. Spread the mix over the bread. Use your hands and/or a spoon and make sure the bread is covered
  6. Put the bread on a baking tray and whack it in the oven
  7. Eat some of the beautiful cheese mix. You should probably keep another portion to snack on.
  8. Fry the eggs in oil and the beautiful cheese mix
  9. Use your judgment to decide when the bread is toasted; I like to burn it slightly at the bottom, but itś really up to you.
  10. Whack the egg onto the bread, and serve after you've remembered to make the Masala Beans
  11. Add a huge thing of polish ketchup on the side
