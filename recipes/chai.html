Chai

What you'll need...

...on hand

  • 2 tablespoons of loos Assam tea
  • 12 slices of ginger
  • 1 and a half teaspoons of black peppercorns
  • 12 cardamom pods
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 5 cloves
  • Three teaspoons of sugar per person
  • 50ml whole milk

...to do

  1. Crush the ingedients in a bowl and add to a saucepan
  2. Add a litre of boiling water and bring to the boil
  3. Heat and simmer until you can smell the spices
  4. Add the sugar and milk, turn up the heat and bring to the boil
  5. Wait for twenty minutes, stirring occasionally
  6. Strain, discard leftover ingredients and servr
